## Scope

- Trenching and pipe installation
- Valve chambers and connection works
- Road reinstatement and housekeeping

## Quality & safety

Controls included bedding and backfill compaction checks, pressure testing, and clear public safety management around open trenches.

## Outcomes

- Improved distribution reliability
- Reduced leak risk through controlled installation practices
- Cleaner reinstatement with reduced community disruption
