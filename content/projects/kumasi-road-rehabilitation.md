## Scope

- Base repair and asphalt overlay works
- Junction geometry improvements and traffic calming
- Kerb, pedestrian walkway, and road marking upgrades

## Delivery approach

Work stages were planned around peak traffic periods with nightly lane closures where required. Material controls included batch verification and compaction testing.

## Outcomes

- Improved ride quality and reduced maintenance needs
- Safer intersections with clearer channelisation
- Better pedestrian separation along high-activity sections
