## Scope

- Internal roads and walkways
- Stormwater drains and culverts

## Delivery approach

Sequencing aligned civils works with utility installation to minimise rework and maintain consistent quality across the estate.

## Outcomes

- Reliable access with improved site circulation
- Drainage network supporting stormwater conveyance
- Clean utility corridors with reduced clash risk
