## Scope

- Drain resectioning, lining, and cover slab replacements
- Culvert rehabilitation and inlet/outlet protection
- Silt traps, debris screens, and maintenance access improvements

## Quality & safety

Works were executed with defined inspection hold points for concrete placement, reinforcement checks, and as-built verification. Public safety controls included barrier systems and clear pedestrian diversions.

## Outcomes

- Improved hydraulic capacity at known bottlenecks
- Reduced erosion at outfalls with improved protection
- Better maintainability through access and debris control
