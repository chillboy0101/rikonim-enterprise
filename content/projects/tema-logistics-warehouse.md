## Scope

- Warehouse substructure and superstructure works
- Heavy-duty concrete yard slab and loading bay detailing
- Surface drainage, service ducts, and utility coordination

## Quality & safety

QA/QC included reinforcement inspections, concrete testing, and dimensional checks. Site safety focused on lifting controls, segregation, and housekeeping.

## Outcomes

- Operational-ready facility with durable yard pavement
- Improved stormwater management through graded falls and drainage
- Coordinated service routes to reduce future disruption
